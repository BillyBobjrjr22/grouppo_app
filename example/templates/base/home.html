About Us

Grouppo is a meeting planner website that facilitates group scheduling and decision-making. We make it easy for you to schedule get-togethers with friends,

family, or colleagues. Input available dates, times, and the place to meet, and our site will pick what works best for everyone. Our goal is to simplify the process of scheduling

and to make it more enjoyable for you. We understand the hassle of organizing a meeting, and we want to help you make it a seamless experience. Our unique feature allows

people to join rooms to participate in the scheduling process, making it even easier to find a time and place that works for everyone. We

are committed to making your life easier by taking the stress out of scheduling meetings. Let us help you plan your next gathering!

Co-created by Nathan Nemetz & Jesse Rehrer
